What it is all about?

March Madness, a very fun sporting event where D1 college basketball teams compete to win a tournament. Just like
it's name, March Madness occurs during March and both men and women compete. There are 4 rounds where teams
compete and the top four teams go into the Final Four and the top 2 teams compete in the National Championship.
The winner of last year in the Men's Championship was UCONN so it's predicted they will win again this year.

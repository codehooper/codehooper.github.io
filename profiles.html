OUR TEAM

Luka Doncic

Luka Doncic is the point guard of the Dallas Mavericks. He averages 33.9 points,
8.7 rebounds and 8.3 assists per game. He averages the most points per game on
the Dallas Mavericks. Currently, they are in the playoffs thanks to him scoring a
clutch 3 pointer, making the mavericks win by one point in Game 2.

Kyrie Irving

Kyrie is the shooting guard of the Dallas Mavericks. He averages 25.6 points,
5.0 rebounds and 5.2 assists per game. He averages the 2nd most points per game on
the Dallas Mavericks. He is known for his crazy good handles and finishing.

Derrick Jones Jr

Derrick Jones Jr is the small foward of the Dallas Mavericks. He averages 8.6 points,
3.3 rebounds and 1.0 assists per game. Although mainly Luka and Kyrie
contribute to the number of points scored Derrick Jones Jr follows all of Lukas plays.

P.J Washington

P.J Washington is the power foward of the Dallas Mavericks. He averages 12.9
points, 5.5 rebounds and 2.2 assists per game. P.J Washington scored
16 points in Game 3 and beat the Timberwolves 116-107.
